## ⚔️ Choose Your Armament

### 🆓 Free Plan — Perfect for Starting Clans
*Built for small squads who just need basic coordination without the automation.*

*   **📅 Basic Operations:** Create up to **1 active operation** at a time with text-based summaries.
*   **📦 Stock Tracking:** Manage up to **2 stockpiles** (limited to 10 core item types per stock) using standard text commands.
*   **🎫 Recruitment Tickets:** Up to **3 open tickets** simultaneously with automated basic roles assignment.
*   **🚨 Basic Emergency:** Text-only emergency alerts with manual channel pings.
*   **📆 Absences (LOA):** Basic registry to track who is away during operations.

*Motivate your logi players and turn the supply grind into a competitive game.*
*   **Automated Merit Points:** Players earn points by delivering crates, refilling bases, and fulfilling frontline requests.

*   **One-Click Operation Sign-ups:** Members join infantry, armor, or logi squads instantly by clicking Discord buttons.
*   **Advanced Supply Chains:** Unlimited stockpiles and items with full lifecycle tracking (Requested ➔ In Transit ➔ Delivered).

#### 📊 War Intelligence & Infrastructure
*   **Tactical Links:** Connect your logistics requests directly to active operations and target objectives.
